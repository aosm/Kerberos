K5Client Support in Kerberos for Macintosh

K5Client was a substitute driver that enabled Eudora to use K5Pop through the KClient API.

K5Client support is not available on Mac OS X.

For information about using it with Keberos for Macintosh 3.5/4.0.x on Mac OS 8.x and 9.x, click here.
