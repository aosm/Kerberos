Kerberos for Macintosh Support & Contact Info

For all problems, questions, and suggestions regarding Kerberos on Mac OS X, you should contact Apple Computer. They will relay questions to MIT as appropriate.

Ways to contact Apple include:

  • The methods listed on the Apple Customer Assistance & Tech Support web page;
  • Filing an incident Apple Developer Technical Support;
  • Filing a bug with Apple via the Apple Developer Connection Bug Reporter;
  • Or if you are at a univeristy or college, your Apple System Engineer may be able to assist you with your questions or help bring them to Apple's attention.

Please only contact MIT direclty for questions regarding the Mac OS X Kerberos Extras, or the documentation on these webpages.

Questions or comments? Send mail to macdev@mit.edu
